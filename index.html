Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Do you actually send the emails, or do you just set up a template for me to use?
We do everything. We write the copy, build the sequence, segment your list, and send it on your behalf. You'll receive a summary report after each campaign. You don't log into any software or touch any settings.
What do you need from me to get started?
Your customer list (exported from your booking software, spreadsheet, or wherever you store it), your shop name, your services and pricing, and 20 minutes of your time for an onboarding call. That's it. Everything after that is on us.
My customer list is messy / old / in a spreadsheet. Will that work?
Absolutely. Most shops we work with have never organized their customer data. We clean and organize the list for you as part of the onboarding process. As long as you have email addresses — even in a rough format — we can work with it.
I've tried email marketing before and it didn't work. Why would this be different?
Generic "email marketing" fails because it's not built around your specific customer relationships. The Customer Revival Campaign uses behavioral segmentation (who came in, what they got, how long ago) and copy that reads like a personal message from you — not a blast. That difference in relevance is the entire reason it works.
How quickly will I see bookings?
Most shops see replies and inbound calls within 48–72 hours of the first campaign going out. Confirmed bookings typically come in within the first 7–14 days. Some shops fill their schedule in the first week alone.
What does this cost?
We discuss pricing on the strategy call once we understand your list size and goals. We don't post pricing publicly because it depends on your volume. What we can tell you: most shops cover the full cost of the service with the revenue from the first campaign alone.
