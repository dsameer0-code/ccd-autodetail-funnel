Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

Do I need a big list for this to work?
Not at all. We've run campaigns for shops with as few as 300 past customers and generated $2,000+ in new bookings. The quality of the relationship matters more than the size of the list.
What if I haven't emailed my customers in a year — or ever?
That's actually where we see the biggest results. "Cold" customer lists often outperform recently emailed ones because those customers haven't been burned out by promotions. A well-crafted re-engagement message hits differently when they haven't heard from you.
How long until I see bookings?
Most shops see inbound responses within 48–72 hours of campaign launch. Most see confirmed bookings within 7–14 days. Some see results within the first 24 hours.
